Description:
The Age Calculator tool helps you determine your age in various units, including years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

User Manual:

  1. Navigate to the "Age Calculator" section.
  2. Enter your birthdate in the provided input field.
  3. Click the "Calculate Age" button.
  4. The tool will display your age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Description:
The Stopwatch tool allows you to measure elapsed time with features such as lap timing, resetting, and stopping.

User Manual:

  1. Access the "Stopwatch" section.
  2. Click the "Start" button to begin measuring time.
  3. Use the "Lap" button to record lap times.
  4. Click the "Stop" button to pause the stopwatch.
  5. Press "Reset" to clear recorded laps and reset the stopwatch.
